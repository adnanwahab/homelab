nanosaur is a simple open-source robot based on NVIDIA Jetson. The robot is fully 3D printable, able to wander on your desk autonomously and uses a simple camera and two OLEDs ‚Äî these act as a pair of eyes. It measures a compact 10x12x6cm and it weighs only 500g.
