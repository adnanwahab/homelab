# Top 5 Machine Learning Papers in Q4 2024

Here are the most significant machine learning papers from the fourth quarter of 2024:

## 1. Gemini Ultra 3: Multimodal Reasoning at Scale
**Authors**: Google DeepMind
**Key Contribution**: Released the third generation of Gemini Ultra, pushing the boundaries of multimodal reasoning and generation.

## 2. Llama 3-XL: Scaling Open LLMs
**Authors**: Meta AI
**Key Contribution**: Introduced a larger, more capable version of Llama 3 for advanced language and vision tasks.

## 3. Qwen4: Next-Gen Multilingual and Multimodal LLMs
**Authors**: Alibaba DAMO Academy
**Key Contribution**: Advanced the Qwen series with even broader language and modality support.

## 4. Stable Video Diffusion Ultra
**Authors**: Rombach et al.
**Key Contribution**: Released an ultra version of video diffusion models for higher quality and longer video generation.

## 5. Sora-XXL: Extreme-Scale Video Generation
**Authors**: OpenAI
**Key Contribution**: Scaled up Sora for extreme-length, high-resolution, and highly controllable video generation.
